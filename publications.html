On the Identifiability of Genetic Parameters for Growth in Mice With a Massively Deep Pedigree

Xi Ding, A. A. Musa, Norbert Reinsch

Animal Breeding and Genetics, 2025

This study investigates the identifiability of genetic parameters in growth models using a massively deep pedigree structure in mice populations, providing insights into parameter estimation accuracy and breeding program optimization. The research addresses critical challenges in quantitative genetics regarding the reliable estimation of variance components in complex breeding populations.
