Peer-Reviewed Publications

On the Identifiability of Genetic Parameters for Growth in Mice With a Massively Deep Pedigree

Xi Ding, A. A. Musa, Norbert Reinsch

Animal Breeding and Genetics, 2025

This study investigates the identifiability of genetic parameters in growth models using a massively deep pedigree structure in mice populations. We address critical challenges in quantitative genetics regarding reliable estimation of variance components in complex breeding populations.

DOI Link

CLARITY: a Shiny app for interactive visualisation of the bovine physical-genetic map

Nina Melzer, Saber Qanbari, Xi Ding, Dörte Wittenburg

Frontiers in Genetics, 2023

We developed CLARITY, an interactive R Shiny application that provides a platform for users to explore bovine genetic and physical maps. The tool allows researchers to visualize the relationship between genetic and physical distances across the bovine genome.
