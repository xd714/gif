Welcome

I am a PhD researcher specializing in computational animal breeding and genetics, with a particular focus on body weight prediction and optimization. My work combines advanced statistical modeling, machine learning techniques, and genomic analysis to improve livestock breeding programs.

Currently based at the Leibniz Institute for Farm Animal Biology (FBN) in Germany, I am passionate about applying computational methods to solve real-world challenges in animal agriculture and sustainable food production.
