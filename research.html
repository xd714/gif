Genetic Parameter Identifiability in Deep Pedigrees

Breakthrough methodology for reliable parameter estimation in complex breeding populations

üéØ The Challenge

Traditional variance component estimation fails in massively deep pedigrees due to identifiability issues. Breeding programs need reliable genetic parameter estimates for accurate selection decisions.

üîß My Solution

Developed novel statistical approaches using mouse populations with 40+ generations. Created diagnostic methods to detect and resolve parameter identifiability problems in BLUP/GBLUP models.
