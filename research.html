Research Focus

I develop computational methods for animal breeding and genetics, combining statistical modeling with machine learning to solve complex challenges in livestock production. My work addresses fundamental questions in quantitative genetics and creates practical tools for breeding programs.

Future Directions

Future work will focus on integrating multi-omics data (genomics, transcriptomics, metabolomics) for comprehensive breeding insights, developing real-time breeding recommendation systems, and creating climate-smart breeding models for resilient livestock production.

I'm interested in collaborative opportunities in computational genetics, data sharing initiatives, and developing open-source tools for the research community.
